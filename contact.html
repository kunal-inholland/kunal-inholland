Fitbit Inspire 3

The Fitbit Inspire 3 is one of the best fitness trackers on the market, especially for its price of € 100. This device has many features that are perfect for tracking your fitness progress, such as sleep tracking, heart rate monitoring, and step counting. Additionally, the Fitbit Inspire 3 boasts a 10-day battery life and water resistance up to 50 meters, making it a great choice for those who want a reliable fitness tracker. Plus, with the Fitbit app you can receive notifications on your smartphone without having to constantly check your device.

Pinetime

Are you tired of your smartwatch not being able to do everything you want it to? Well, Pinetime is the watch for you! This watch is specifically designed for developers who want to extend its functionality. You can order a dev kit and use it to add all sorts of new features to the watch. Plus, at only € 30, it's a great value for anyone who wants to get the most out of their smartwatch.

MI Band 6

Are you on a tight budget but still want a quality fitness tracker? Look no further than the MI Band 6. This affordable tracker has an impressive AMOLED display and 30 different workout trackers to choose from. You can also use it to regulate your breathing, set reminders, and receive calls and notifications from your smartphone. Best of all, it only costs € 40. So don't miss out on this great deal!
